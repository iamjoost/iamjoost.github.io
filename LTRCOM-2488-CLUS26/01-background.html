Background

Cisco Intersight provides a portfolio of services that enables intelligent visualization, optimization, and orchestration to connect your teams, tools, infrastructure, and applications.

Cisco UCS X-Series servers work with Cisco Intersight to deploy and run virtualized and non-virtualized applications. This results in better performance and operational efficiency. There are many benefits to managing Cisco UCS X-Series with Cisco Intersight.

  • Enhanced Management Capabilities: Cisco Intersight provides a modern and intuitive management interface that simplifies managing Cisco UCS X-Series blade servers. It offers a centralized management platform that allows administrators to monitor, configure, and troubleshoot their infrastructure from anywhere using a web-based interface.
  • Visibility and Analytics: Cisco Intersight provides advanced monitoring and analytic capabilities, giving administrators deep visibility into the performance and health of their blade servers. It offers real-time data and analytics, allowing proactive identification of potential issues and the ability to make informed decisions to optimize the system's performance.
  • Automation and Orchestration: Cisco Intersight enables automation and orchestration of routine tasks and operations. It provides policy-based management, allowing administrators to define policies once and apply them consistently across the infrastructure. This automation helps streamline operations, reduce manual errors, and improve overall efficiency.
  • Integration with Cloud Services: Cisco Intersight integrates seamlessly with Cisco cloud-based services. This integration enables workload optimization, capacity planning, and cost optimization, helping customers make the most efficient use of their resources and maximize their ROI.
  • Simplified Firmware Management: Cisco Intersight simplifies the process of managing firmware updates for Cisco UCS X-Series blade servers. It provides a centralized firmware management interface, making it easy to keep the system up to date with the latest firmware releases and security patches.
  • Future-Proof Investment: Cisco actively invests in Cisco Intersight and introducing new features and capabilities. By migrating to Cisco Intersight, customers ensure that they are using the latest innovations and taking advantage of ongoing updates and improvements.
  • Support for Hybrid and Multi-Cloud Environments: Cisco designed Cisco Intersight to support hybrid and multicloud environments. This provides customers with a unified management experience across their entire infrastructure, whether it's on-premises, in the cloud, or a combination of both. This flexibility lets customers embrace hybrid cloud strategies and seamlessly manage their resources across different environments.
